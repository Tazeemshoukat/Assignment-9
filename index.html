3. Glossary Example

Biology/Computer-Science
Biology is the study of living organisms, their structure, function, growth, and evolution.
Computer Science is the study of computation, programming, and the design of systems.
Chemistry
Chemistry is the branch of science that deals with the composition and properties of substances.
It studies elements, compounds, reactions, and energy changes.
Physics
Physics is the study of matter, motion, energy, and the fundamental laws of nature.
It explains how the universe behaves at microscopic and macroscopic levels.
